Spook!

The game of collecting the humans. Now in alpha.

Ghost Controls:
w-a-s-d For the respective movement(a-d, on ladders use w-s)
z to jump
x to get onto ladders
c to reset your character to spawn

Daemon Controls:
Arrow Keys For the respective movement(L-R, on ladders use U-D)
7 to jump
8 to get onto ladders
9 to reset your character to spawn
