Title: Square OffDescription: Square off against a friend and attempt to capture their flag! Use your weapon to gain the upper hand, but be wary, capturing the flag will not be easy. Not only are you facing off against another player, but also the environment. Will you be the winner? First player to capture three flags wins! Credits: Kevin Hickson—Lead programmer, project manager, lead level designer.Nicolas Pinton—Lead artist, level designer, lead character artist.EJ Gillis—Level designer, producer
