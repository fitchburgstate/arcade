"Bear - Handed" Prison Break

"Bear - Handed" Prison Break is a 2 player co-op game where they must work in unison to escape a
cursed prison. Player 1 must use their short ranged attacks along with Player 2’s long ranged
attacks to navigate their way past the angry warden and out of the prison.

Josh Mulligan - Art, sound design, level design
Brandon McKearney - Programmer, leve design
Nathan Timbro - Producer, Programmer
Chris Banks - Character Art, Animation
